. The Phoney War and German Invasion

The Phoney War

After the Germans invaded Poland France and the UK declared war but didn't dare invade for fear of a repeat of the First World War.This was called 'The Phoney War' where no-one dared move

But the French decided to make the first move called 'The Saar Offensive' but gave up after a few days. After this There was some stuff other stuff in Northern Europe but after that the Phoney war was over.

German Invasion

During the Phoney war the Allies took to long to make a move and that allowed Hitler to have his army ready for the Invasion.

During the Inter war periods France put together a series of forts along the Franco-Prussian border called 'The Maginot Line' that was thought to be impenetrable but Germany had a better idea.

The Germans punched through Belgium, the Netherlands and Luxembourg coping what they did years earlier with 'The Schlieffen Plan'
